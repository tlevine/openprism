  • data.gov.uk

  • DataHub

    DataHub is a store of open datasets that are free to use.

  • GeoNames

    GeoNames is integrating geographical data such as names of places in various languages, elevation, population and others from various sources.

  • GIST

    The Government Interrogating Spending Tool

  • Global Entrepreneurship Monitor Data

    The GEM project is an annual assessment of the entrepreneurial activity, aspirations and attitudes of individuals across a wide range of countries.

  • Lexvo

    Lexvo brings information about languages, words, characters, and other human language-related entities to the Linked Data Web and Semantic Web.

  • NetIndex

    NetIndex is a publicly available dataset of broadband and mobile speed and quality test results.

  • OpenlyLocal

    OpenlyLocal colates information on how open local councils are.

    Virtually combines Global LAM (Library Archives Museum) name authority files into a single name authority service.
